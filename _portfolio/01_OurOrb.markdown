I founded <a href="https://ourorb.energy/">OurOrb Energy</a> in 2026 to bring plug-in solar to more people. We are building the digital infrastructure for plug-in solar in the UK. Our aim is to help to further democratise renewable energy.

The platform includes a range of tools. For homes we launched a <a href="https://ourorb.energy/calculator">Solar Calculator</a> and a <a href="https://ourorb.energy/dno">DNO Lookup</a>. Alongside the consumer tools, we provide other software and data for businesses such as our <a href="https://ourorb.energy/api">APIs</a> and <a href="https://ourorb.energy/widgets">widgets</a>.
