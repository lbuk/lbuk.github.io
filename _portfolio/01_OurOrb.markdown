I founded <a href="https://ourorb.energy/">OurOrb Energy</a> in 2026 to bring plug-in solar to more people. We are building solar tools and resources that will empower homes and businesses in the UK and help to democratise renewable energy.

The platform includes a <a href="https://ourorb.energy/calculator">Solar Calculator</a> and a <a href="https://ourorb.energy/dno">DNO Lookup</a> for homes. Alongside the consumer tools, we provide <a href="https://ourorb.energy/api">APIs</a> and <a href="https://ourorb.energy/widgets">widgets</a> for businesses - installers, retailers, platforms etc - to add plug-in solar guidance to their own sites.
