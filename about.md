<div class="description">
	<p>Specialising in data visualisation, spatial data science and GIS, I've developed a number of novel tools such as the <a href="https://www.liamthomasbolton.com/portfolio/FresnelMap/">Fresnel Map</a>, <a href="https://www.github.com/lbuk/fmap">fmap</a>, <a href="https://www.liamthomasbolton.com/portfolio/SpaceRatio/">Space Ratio and the Space Ratio Chart</a>.</p>
	<p>I'm currently a PhD Architectural Space and Computation student at <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/bartlett/">The Bartlett</a>, UCL. My <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/bartlett/architecture/liam-thomas-bolton">PhD research</a> explores techniques for mapping and measuring airspace development in London.</p>
    <p>In the past, I've collaborated with research labs, local authorities, government departments, non-profits, Innovation Labs and news agencies. I completed an MSc in Smart Cities and Urban Analytics at the <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/bartlett/casa/">Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis (CASA)</a>, UCL, and a BA (Hons) in Social Policy from the <a href="https://www.leeds.ac.uk">University of Leeds</a>. I've given talks at <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/bartlett/casa/">CASA</a>, the <a href="https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/government-digital-service/">Government Digital Service</a>, the <a href="https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/department-for-transport/">Department for Transport</a> and the launch of the Leeds City Dashboard. I've also been funded by <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/">UCL</a>, the <a href="https://www.thebritishacademy.ac.uk/">British Academy</a> and Eliahou Dangoor.</p>
    <p>Prior to starting my PhD I worked as a Consultant for <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/bartlett/architecture/bartlett-school-architecture">The Bartlett School of Architecture</a> on a data project. Between 2017 and 2018 I worked as an Data Analyst at <a href="http://www.redninja.co.uk/">Red Ninja Studios</a>, a design-led technology company, and <a href="https://isensing.co.uk/">iSensing</a>, an IoT company that provides mobility analytics. Here, I led a planning project and visualised real-time data in Madrid and Sao Paulo. After graduating from the University of Leeds in 2017, I worked with the <a href="https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/department-for-transport/">Department for Transport</a> to explore how machine learning and big data can be used to model car dependency with the <a href="https://lida.leeds.ac.uk/">Leeds Institute for Data Analytics</a>. In 2016 I was awarded a Google Fellowship at <a href="https://bbcnewslabs.co.uk">BBC News Labs</a> and the Graphics desk where I contributed to data-driven stories, helped with the production of news applications and worked on a project to encourage trust in news. I've collaborated with <a href="https://www.graphicnews.com/">Graphic News</a>, a news agency specialising in infographics and data visualisation, and the <a href="https://www.rgs.org/">Royal Geographical Society</a>. As ex-Founder of <a href="https://github.com/studentdatalabs">Student Data Labs</a>, I managed Innovation Labs between 2015 and 2016 that aimed to teach practical data skills to university students while tackling civic problems.</p>
