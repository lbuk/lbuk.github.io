<div class="description">
	<p>Specialising in the built environment, design and data, I recently completed a PhD in Architectural Space and Computation at <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/bartlett/architecture/bartlett-school-architecture">The Bartlett School of Architecture</a>, UCL. My <a href="https://discovery.ucl.ac.uk/id/eprint/10192942/">PhD thesis</a> explored techniques for mapping and measuring airspace development in London. I also developed a number of novel tools such as the <a href="https://www.liamthomasbolton.com/portfolio/FresnelMap/">Fresnel Map</a>, <a href="https://www.github.com/lbuk/fmap">fmap</a>, <a href="https://www.liamthomasbolton.com/portfolio/SpaceRatio/">Space Ratio and the Space Ratio Chart</a>.</p>
    <p>I've worked on projects with <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/bartlett/architecture/bartlett-school-architecture">The Bartlett School of Architecture</a>, the <a href="https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/department-for-transport/">Department for Transport</a>, the <a href="https://lida.leeds.ac.uk/">Leeds Institute for Data Analytics</a> at the University of Leeds, <a href="https://bbcnewslabs.co.uk">BBC News Labs</a> as a Google Fellow and <a href="https://www.leeds.gov.uk/">Leeds City Council</a> Innovation Labs. As ex-Founder of <a href="https://github.com/studentdatalabs">Student Data Labs</a>, I managed Innovation Labs that aimed to teach practical data skills to university students while tackling civic problems. I completed an MSc in Smart Cities and Urban Analytics at the <a href="https://www.ucl.ac.uk/bartlett/casa/">Centre for Advanced Spatial Analysis (CASA)</a> at UCL and a BA (Hons) in Social Policy from the <a href="https://www.leeds.ac.uk">University of Leeds</a>.</p>
