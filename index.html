About

I'm a specialist in spatial data science, data visualisation and research. I've invented a few new techniques and published on a range of subjects.

I've worked with Google, The Bartlett School of Architecture, the Leeds Institute for Data Analytics, BBC News Labs and Leeds City Council Innovation Labs.

I'm also passionate about the built environment and sustainability, having completed a PhD in Architectural Space and Computation at UCL. My thesis explored techniques for measuring and mapping rooftop housing.
