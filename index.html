About

I'm the Founder of OurOrb Energy. We're helping homes and businesses reduce their energy bills and carbon emissions by building the digital infrastructure for plug-in solar.

I'm passionate about the built environment and sustainability, having completed a PhD in Architectural Space and Computation at UCL. My thesis explored how London is making use of underutilised spaces for new housing. In the process, I created new techniques for measuring and mapping rooftop housing.

In the past, I've worked with Google, The Bartlett School of Architecture, the Leeds Institute for Data Analytics, BBC News Labs and a range of environmental organisations.
